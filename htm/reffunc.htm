Bullet 3.0 has no initialization needs. That's right. Bullet 3.0 is ready to go without any initialization. Bullet was designed to be fully reentrant and to be thread-safe when used correctly. Any number of processes may be inside Bullet at any one time, and any number of threads in those processes may be within Bullet -- the first thread, the second, or the 100th thread, any or all -- at the same time.
